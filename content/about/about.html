Kyle Machulis, better known as qDot, is a graduate of the University of Oklahoma with a degree in Computer Science. He currently spends his days as a mild-mannered engineer, and his nights sitting in front of his computer with the firm belief that someone on the internet cares about what he does.

Kyle has worked in everything from sex and technology to health devices to self driving cars to device reverse engineering and more (see the portfolio page for a more complete list). If there was some unifying aspect to all of these projects that he was aware of, it would be explained here. However, best he can figure, the only thread through all of the projects he's worked on was that he was the one that worked on them.

At least, he thinks he was the one that worked on them.
