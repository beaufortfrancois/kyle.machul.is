Qt based client for the omron pedometer for [Shape Up The Nation](http://shapeupthenation.com).
Allows SUTN members to plug their pedometer into a computer and have
information instantly uploaded to SUTN's cloud services. Uses the
[libomron library](http://github.com/openyou/libomron), also developed by Nonpolynomial Labs.
